The Spread of Covid-19 Cases Worldwide

Our project will be utilizing the COVID dataset from CORGIS. The plan is to create a map of the world, which can be used as a choropleth map to show the density of COVID in various counties at various points in time. Selecting countries from the map can trigger the drawing of innnovation chart charts and visualizations. Users can interate with Day, Month, Year and Attribute, data will be pllotted in scatterplot and map chart. The circles in Circle chart can be draged and represented the days when death cases were above 1000.
